Our Story

Blondie Brownie began in 2018 in our family kitchen in Yorkshire. What started as weekend baking sessions with our grandmother's recipes quickly blossomed into something special when friends and neighbors couldn't get enough of our treats.

Today, our small family business continues to use those same cherished recipes, with each brownie and blondie still mixed, baked, and packaged by hand. We source the finest ingredients locally whenever possible and take pride in creating treats that bring joy to our community.

Our café opened in 2021, creating a warm space where people could enjoy our baked goods alongside expertly crafted beverages in a cozy, welcoming environment.
